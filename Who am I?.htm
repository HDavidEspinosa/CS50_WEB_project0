BIO

My name is Hernan David Espinosa. I am I PhD student in Melbourne, Australia. Originally, I am from Colombia. I studied music on the hopes of becoming a producer, which resulted in me becoming a Sound Design and Audio Editing teacher. This in turn got me to get my MA in screenwriting to become a creator… which got me really interested in stories. This led me to teaching and studying …hmmmm… everything! As part of a communications faculty I started studying everything about telling stories in our time. Which is a huge subject I love getting lost into. But to narrow it a bit I focus on:
